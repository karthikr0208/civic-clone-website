The Civic
Drive

Engineered for the road ahead

The Civic ZX rides on an independent multi‑link rear suspension paired with MacPherson struts up front, delivering a composed, planted feel at every speed. With 171 mm of ground clearance and 17‑inch alloy wheels, it handles urban roads and open highways with equal confidence.

Vehicle Stability Assist (VSA), Agile Handle Assist (AHA), and Hill Start Assist (HSA) work quietly in the background — so you focus on the drive, not the road conditions.
